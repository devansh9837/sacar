- Label: Core Advisory Services
- Heading: **"Three Integrated Practice Areas"**
- Subtitle: "Delivering diplomatic intelligence across the space domain — from norm-building to strategic foresight."

### Pillar 1: Diplomatic Strategy & Norm-Building
- Summary: "Navigating the soft power of space — creating international norms and diplomatic frameworks that govern celestial behavior."
- Sub-services (expandable):
  - **Multilateral Engagement Support** — "Researching historical precedents and current trends to support delegations in international forums (e.g., UN COPUOS)."
  - **Track II Diplomacy** — "Facilitating informal, academic-led dialogues between international stakeholders to find common ground outside of formal state channels."
  - **Space Policy Alignment** — "Analyzing how a nation's or organization's space objectives align with — or disrupt — existing international diplomatic trends."

### Pillar 2: Geopolitical Risk & Macro-Forecasting
- Summary: "Using deep-history and political-science frameworks to provide long-range outlooks on the New Space Race."
- Sub-services (expandable):
  - **Alliance Mapping** — "Detailed research on emerging space blocs (e.g., the Artemis Accords vs. the ILRS) and their implications for global trade and technology transfer."
  - **Geopolitical Impact Reports** — "Analyzing how terrestrial conflicts (e.g., Arctic competition or South China Sea tensions) are manifesting in space policy."
  - **Strategic Foresight** — "Developing 10- and 20-year Alternative Futures for the space economy based on current geopolitical shifts."

### Pillar 3: Space Policy & Governance Research
- Summary: "Extensive research into the political intent behind governance structures and how they are interpreted globally."
- Sub-services (expandable):
  - **Comparative Policy Analysis** — "Academic reviews of how different nations (emerging vs. established space powers) approach orbital debris, resource extraction, and peaceful use."
  - **Incentive Structure Analysis** — "Researching how to design international cooperation models that encourage transparency and discourage unilateral escalation."
  - **Public Policy Advisory** — "Helping organizations draft white papers and position statements grounded in rigorous, peer-reviewed evidence."
